No purchase necessary to enter or win. A purchase or payment of any kind will not improve your chance of winning.

Employees, officers, directors, agents and representatives of ESPN Internet Ventures ("Sponsor"), teamDigital Promotions, Inc. ("teamDigital" and/or "Administrator"), Warner Bros. Entertainment, Inc., Legend Pictures LLC., Toho Co., Ltd. , Hilton Worldwide, and each of their respective parent companies, subsidiaries, affiliates, partnerships, advertising/promotion/fulfillment agencies, anyone else connected with the production and distribution of this Sweepstakes (hereafter "Released Parties") and their immediate families (spouse, parent, child, sibling and their respective spouses, regardless of where they reside) and those living in their same household, whether or not related, are not eligible to enter or win. Sweepstakes is void where prohibited by law. To enter the Sweepstakes, entrant must be a member of ESPN.com or one of Sponsor's family of sites (e.g., ABC.com, ABCNEWS.com, Disney Online Family.com). Registration is free. If entrant is not a member, entrant may register by clicking on the link as indicated on the Sweepstakes entry page and following the instructions. During registration, entrant will be asked to provide his or her name, physical address, zip code, telephone number, email address, birth date and gender, select a user name and password and accept the Terms of Use on ESPN.com. Those who are already members of ESPN.com will be asked to enter their account information (email address or user name and password) to enter. Entrant may then be asked: (i) to answer survey questions or (ii) to consent to receive promotional emails from the Sponsor. Answering survey questions or consenting to receive promotional emails is optional and does not have to be agreed to in order to be eligible to enter the Sweepstakes or to win a prize, nor does answering survey questions or consenting to receive promotional emails increase an entrant's chances of winning. As a member, entrant may enter the Sweepstakes by logging in to the system with entrant's current username and password. Entrants are responsible for insuring that all contact information is up-to-date and accurate.

To Participate

Visit www.espn.com, and search for the keyword Godzilla between 12:00 PM Eastern Time ("ET") on 4/18/14 and 11:59 AM ET on 5/18/14 (the "Promotion Period") and complete the member sign-in to receive one (1) entry. There is a limit of one (1) entry per person/household/email address per day. Additional entries from any person, household or email address in excess of the stated limitation will be void. Incomplete and inaccurate entries are void. The authorized account holder is defined as the natural person who is assigned an e-mail address by an Internet access provider, an online service provider, or another organization that is responsible for assigning e-mail addresses or the domain associated with the submitted e-mail address. Proof (to Sponsor's satisfaction) of being the authorized account holder may be required by Sponsor.

Winner Selection

The potential Grand Prize winner will be selected in a random drawing conducted on or about 5/20/14 from among all eligible entries received by 11:59 PM ET on 5/18/14. The results of the random drawing will be final and binding in all matters relating to this Sweepstakes. Winner will be notified on or about 5/21/14 by email, telephone, overnight delivery and/or mail. Odds of winning will depend on the total number of eligible entries received.

One (1) Grand Prize

A trip for the Grand Prize winner and one (1) guests to Oahu, Hawaii. Trip consists of round-trip coach air transportation from a major airport nearest to winner's residence in the U.S. (as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion) to destination airport, one (1) standard room double-occupancy hotel accommodations for five (5) nights, ground transportation to/from the airport and the applicable hotel and two (2) tickets to the Waikiki Starlight Luau. Approximate Retail Value ("ARV") of Grand Prize package: $4,500. Airline carrier and hotel regulations and conditions apply. Airline tickets will be awarded in the form of a Hawaiian Airlines voucher.  Winner has ninety (90) days to activate the voucher and has eighteen (18) months from voucher activation date to redeem the miles. Travel must be made through Sponsor's agent. Once the travel schedule has been arranged, it cannot be altered and failure of winner to follow such schedule shall not obligate Sponsor in any way to provide the winner with alternate arrangements. Released Parties are not responsible for airline cancellations or delays. Sponsor, in its sole discretion, reserves the right to provide ground transportation in place of air transportation if winner lives within a 100 mile radius of Oahu, Hawaii, as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion, and to award no additional compensation or substitution for the difference in prize value in such event. Winner and his/her respective guest must travel together on the same itinerary and are responsible for obtaining any/all necessary travel documents (including but not limited to valid photo I.D.) prior to departure. If the actual value of the prize is less than the stated ARV, the difference will not be awarded. Winner may be required to provide a credit card at time of hotel check-in to cover hotel incidentals. Any depiction of prize is for illustrative purposes only. Prize elements may not be separated. Airline tickets issued in conjunction with the prize are not eligible for frequent flyer miles, and no code share flights may be used. All prize components are based on availability and restrictions apply.
